# Method Pre-Writing Questions

Before writing Method, answer:

1. What task formulation does the method assume?
2. What exact failure or limitation does the method address?
3. What modules or stages does the method contain?
4. For each module:
   - What input does it receive?
   - What workflow does it follow?
   - What output does it produce?
   - Why is it needed?
   - Why does it work?
5. What loss, reward, objective, or algorithm trains the method?
6. How does the method produce final recommendations?
7. What extra inference or training cost does it introduce?

Recommended action:

1. Organize answers in a table before writing paragraphs.
2. Map each table row to one Method subsection.
